**Name**: CryptoTick & Alert - Real-time Price Monitor
**Summary**: Real-time crypto prices on extension icon, volatility alerts, and candlestick charts. Supports Binance & OKX.

**Description**:

ğŸš€ **Never Miss a Crypto Move Again!**

CryptoTick & Alert is a professional browser extension designed for traders and hodlers who need to stay updated without constantly switching tabs.

**Key Features:**

* **Real-time Badge Price**: View live prices (BTC, ETH, SOL, DOGE) directly on your browser toolbar icon. Green for up, red for down.
* **Smart Volatility Alerts**: Set custom thresholds (e.g., "Alert me if BTC moves 2% in 1 minute").
* **Distinct Audio Cues**: Hear the difference between a Pump (Sharp Rise) and a Dump (Deep Fall) instantly.
* **Interactive K-Line Chart**: Built-in professional candlestick chart with multiple timeframes (1m, 5m, 15m, 1h, 4h, 1d).
* **Dual Exchange Support**: Toggle between Binance and OKX data sources seamlessly.
* **Pin Mode**: Detach the popup into a minimalist "Always on Top" window for dedicated monitoring.
* **Privacy First & Open Source**: No tracking, no analytics. The extension is open-source and connects directly to public exchange APIs. [View on GitHub](https://github.com/Ethantao/CryptoTick_Extension)

**Perfect for:**

* Day traders tracking 1-minute volatility.
* DeFi users watching ETH gas or SOL price action.
* Anyone who wants to keep an eye on the market while working.
